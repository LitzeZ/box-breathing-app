Privacy Policy for Lung

Last Updated: January 30, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Lung ("we", "our") handles your information.

1. No Data Collection

We do not collect, store, share, or sell any personal data. Lung is a completely private, offline-first application.

2. Local Storage

Preferences (like theme or breathing patterns) are stored locally on your device via Apple's UserDefaults system. This data never leaves your device.

3. Contact

For questions, please contact us via our support page.
